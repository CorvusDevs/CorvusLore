# Lore

A private memory for the people in your life. Capture what you know about
friends and family, likes and dislikes, gift ideas, important dates, and how
each person likes to be supported, and have it resurface when you need it.

Built for people who find it hard to hold all of that social detail in their
head, including ADHD and autistic users. Local first, on device, nothing leaves
your phone unless you turn on iCloud sync.

Universal SwiftUI app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. See `docs/superpowers/specs`
for the design spec and `docs/superpowers/plans` for the build plans.
