Remember the people
who matter most

Lore keeps rich notes on the people in your life. Likes, dislikes, important dates, conversation topics, a social playbook for how to support them, gift ideas, and more. Fully private, fully on-device.

Download on the App Store iOS, iPadOS, and Mac. No account required.
Features

Everything you wish you could remember

Rich profiles

Keep everything about a person in one place: their loves, their dislikes, inside jokes, topics to avoid, pronouns, dietary needs, relationship notes, and anything else that helps you show up well for them.

Social playbook

Write a personal guide for how to support someone: what helps when they are struggling, how they like to communicate, what they need from you. A quiet cheat-sheet for being a better friend or family member.

Frictionless capture

Log a thought by text, voice, or photo and attach it to a person in seconds. No rigid forms. When you remember something mid-conversation, jot it down before it slips away.

Gentle reminders

Birthdays, anniversaries, "check in on them after the job interview" -- Lore sends quiet nudges at the right moment so important things do not slip through the cracks.

Gift ideas

Note gift ideas as they come to you throughout the year and link them to a person. When a birthday arrives you already have a list, not a blank mind under pressure.

On-device AI

Ask for a briefing before catching up with someone, get a suggestion for a topic they would love, or let Lore spot a pattern in your notes. All AI runs on your device and sends nothing to a server.

Your data stays on your device

Lore has no account, no servers, and no cloud sync. Every profile, every note, every playbook lives only on the device in your hands. The developer cannot see it. No one can. That is the point.

Built for how your brain works

Designed with ADHD and autistic users in mind. Low friction, no judgment, no social pressure. Just a quiet place to remember the people you love.
